1. Note About Children

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of data provided by a child, we will delete it as soon as possible. Parents or guardians who believe we may have collected data from their child should contact us.

2. Legal Bases for Data Processing

TBH Digital Solutions processes your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: Where you have provided clear and explicit consent for us to process your data for specific purposes, such as email marketing.
  • Contract Fulfillment: Processing is necessary for the performance of a contract, such as providing our services or CRM tools as requested by you.
  • Legitimate Interest: We process data for our legitimate interests, such as improving services, fraud prevention, or personalized marketing, balanced against user privacy rights.
  • Legal Obligation: Processing is necessary for compliance with legal obligations, including tax, fraud prevention, and regulatory requirements.

3. How We Collect Information

We collect both Personal Data and Anonymous Data through various means, including direct input, cookies, and third-party services.

Types of Information Collected

  • Personal Data: Data identifying you as an individual (e.g., name, email, IP address).
  • Anonymous Data: Data not directly identifying an individual, such as device type and general usage patterns.

Collection Methods

  • Direct Input: Information provided by users via account creation, forms, or surveys.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Including pixels, beacons, and cookies.
  • Third-Party Integrations: Information collected by analytics, CRM, and advertising partners.

4. Data Retention Policy

We retain personal data as long as it is necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, unless otherwise required by law. Retention periods include:

  • Account Information: Retained until account deactivation and for up to 7 years thereafter as required by legal, regulatory, or contractual obligations.
  • Analytics Data: Retained for a period of 2 years to monitor performance and improve services.
  • Marketing Data: Retained until the user opts out or withdraws consent.
  • Legal and Compliance Data: Retained as needed to meet legal requirements and enforce our policies.

Upon user request, we will delete or anonymize data as permitted by law.

5. Use of Your Personal Data

TBH Digital Solutions uses your data for advanced marketing, engagement, and security purposes, including:

  • Audience Segmentation: Using demographic and behavioral data for targeted marketing.
  • Behavioral Analytics: Monitoring interactions to improve content strategies.
  • Automated CRM Campaigns: Tailoring content based on engagement history.
  • Email and SMS Marketing: Communicating high-impact offers with user consent.
  • Remarketing and Personalized Ads: Targeting ads to users on other websites.

6. Data Storage and Security Measures

We implement industry-standard encryption, access controls, and secure transmission protocols. Data is stored primarily in the U.S., with regular backups to ensure continuity.

  • Encryption Standards: 256-bit SSL encryption for data in transit.
  • Access Control: Data access is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Backups and Redundancy: Data is regularly backed up in secure storage.

7. Disclosure of Your Personal Data

We may share your data under the following conditions:

  • With Your Consent: When you opt-in to data sharing.
  • Service Providers: Trusted providers for CRM, analytics, and customer support.
  • Legal Requirements: As required by law or to protect TBH Digital Solutions’ rights.

Users have the following rights:

Detailed Cookie Usage

1. Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are required for site functionality, such as page navigation, secure logins, and data protection. Without these cookies, services like account access and navigation may not be possible. These cookies are session-based and do not retain personal information.

2. Performance Cookies

Performance cookies help us improve our website by collecting information about how visitors use it. This includes tracking pages visited, time spent on the site, and identifying any errors encountered. Performance cookies enable us to optimize the site for better speed and functionality.

Example:
- _ga (Google Analytics): Used to distinguish unique users by assigning a random ID. Duration: 2 years.

3. Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies provide insights into user behavior on our site, such as traffic sources, popular content, and session duration. These cookies aid in refining the user experience and tailoring content to meet users' needs.

Example:
- _gid (Google Analytics): Tracks visitor behavior and provides reports on site usage. Duration: 24 hours.

4. Marketing Cookies

Marketing cookies track browsing habits and allow us to serve relevant ads based on user interests. They also help measure the effectiveness of ad campaigns. These cookies support remarketing, enabling us to show ads to users on other sites after they have visited ours.

Examples:
- fr (Facebook): Enables ad retargeting on Facebook and Instagram. Duration: 3 months.
- IDE (Google Ads): Tracks ad interactions and measures ad performance. Duration: 13 months.

5. Social Media Cookies

Social media cookies enable sharing content directly on social media platforms and may collect user information for targeting ads on third-party platforms. These cookies help us understand how users engage with our content on social media and improve our ad campaigns.

Example:
- _fbp (Facebook Pixel): Tracks website visits for personalized ads on Facebook. Duration: 3 months.

6. Preferences Cookies

Preference cookies allow us to remember choices you make on our website, such as language settings or region, for a more personalized experience.

Example:
- language: Stores the user’s preferred language for a consistent browsing experience. Duration: 1 year.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie settings at any time by adjusting your browser settings. You may also choose to opt out of specific third-party cookies through their respective platforms (e.g., Google Ads, Facebook).
